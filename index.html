Lobia | Black Eyed Beans Curry

Description

Ingredients 2 Cups Lobia 2 Medium Onions (Chopped Finely) 1 Tbsp Ginger paste 2 Tbsp Garlic paste 2 Tbsp Chili flakes 2 Tsp Degi mirch powder 2 Tsp Kashmir Mirch Powder 2 Tsp Turmeric powder 1 Tsp Dhania powder 1 Tsp Jeera powder 1 Tsp Nutmeg powder 1 Tsp Dagad phool/Stone flower powder 1-2 Tsp or as per taste Salt 1.5 Cups Thick tomato purée 1 Tsp Sugar or Honey (Optional) 1 handful Coriander greens Chopped roughly)

Cooking

1

Wash the Lobia in a large bowl. Pour boiling hot water. Cover. Let it soak for an hour. Pressure cook on high with a teaspoon of salt, a teaspoon of turmeric & 6 cups of water for 7-8 minutes. Let the pressure release automatically.

2

In the meantime, prepare the masala. Heat oil in a skillet or wok. Tip in the chili flakes. Sauté the onion until golden. Add the ginger & garlic paste. Cook for a minute. Pour in the tomato purée. Add all the dry ingredients. Fry vigorously (bhuno) the masala until oil releases & it comes together nicely. (This is important for a nice red color).

3

Add it to the boiled Lobia along with the coriander greens. Let it simmer covered for about 10 minutes. Adjust seasoning. Add sugar or honey (if using. I like it slightly sweet as it balances the flavors pretty well). Serve with piping hot Steamed Rice or Jeera Pulao. Bon Appètit!
